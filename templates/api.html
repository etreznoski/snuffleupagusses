The important information we pulled was the total population, median income, education levels, and population with access to high-speed internet. Data Structure

This graphics below show the structure of our demographic end points.

States Demographics

To access the endpoints for all 50 states, simply add /api/states/ to the end of the URL.

Cities Demographics

To access the endpoints for each individual city, simply add /api/cities/stateabbreviation (ex. /api/cities/OR) to the end of the URL.
